About the console

In order to use visual studios, you have do download it. Once you do that you will have to accept the agreements. In order to open a file, you make a folder. Then you click open folder and click on the folder you created. After that you click new file. After that, you are free to create your app!

The console is used so that you have direct acces to the files. It allows console apps to run inside a window

The console is a place you can go to when you inspect a page. You get to it when you right click, and then you press inspect. At the top of what opens, you will see console. Click on it and then you are there.
